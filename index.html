Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit?
As much or as little as you want! This is a flexible program designed to work around your schedule.

No! We welcome all skill levels. If you're new to coding, we can help you learn.

No problem! You can take breaks whenever needed or leave the program entirely.
